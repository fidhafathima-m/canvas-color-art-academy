About Canvas & Color Art Academy - Kannur's Leading Art Institute

We opened our doors in 2005 because we believe every child has an artist inside them. Our studio in Kannur is a space where kids aged 5 to 16 can explore paints, pencils, and clay without worrying about making mistakes.

Why Choose Our Art Academy in Kannur?

Drawing & Painting Classes in Kannur

We teach the basics - how to hold a brush, mix colors, understand light and shadow. Kids start with simple exercises and gradually work up to creating their own pieces.

Building Confidence & Focus

When a child finishes a drawing they're proud of, something clicks. They realize they can create something from nothing.

Creative Thinking & Problem Solving

Art pushes kids to think outside the box. How do you show movement in a still image? What happens when you mix these two colors?
